Egusi Soup

Egusi Soup is a popular Nigerian soup made with ground melon seeds (egusi), vegetables, and assorted meats, served with pounded yam, fufu, or eba.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • 2 cups assorted meats (beef, goat meat, or tripe)
  • 2 cups spinach or ugu leaves (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp palm oil
  • 2 tbsp ground crayfish
  • 1 tsp salt

Preparation Steps:

  1. Heat palm oil in a pot, add chopped onions, and fry for a few seconds.
  2. Add the ground egusi and stir continuously for a few minutes until slightly thickened.
  3. Pour in the meat stock and stir, allowing the egusi to cook properly.
  4. Add the assorted meats, crayfish, seasoning cubes, salt, and ground pepper, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in the chopped vegetables, cook for a few more minutes, and serve with pounded yam or fufu.
