Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice is a classic Nigerian dish made with rice cooked in a rich tomato sauce, flavored with spices, and often served with fried plantains, chicken, or beef.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 4 large tomatoes (blended)
  • 2 red bell peppers (blended)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cups chicken or beef stock
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp thyme

Preparation Steps:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pot, saut√© onions, then add blended tomatoes, bell peppers, and tomato paste. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  2. Add chicken or beef stock, season with thyme, curry powder, salt, seasoning cubes, and cayenne pepper. Stir well.
  3. Rinse the rice and add it to the pot. Mix well, ensuring the rice absorbs the sauce.
  4. Add enough water to cook the rice, cover the pot, and let it simmer on low heat until fully cooked.
  5. Stir, fluff the rice, and serve with fried plantains, chicken, or salad.
