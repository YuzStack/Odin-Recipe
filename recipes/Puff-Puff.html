Puff-Puff

Puff-Puff is a Nigerian deep-fried dough snack, soft and fluffy on the inside with a slightly crispy outer layer.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1½ tsp dry yeast
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 cup warm water
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Preparation Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix the yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add flour and salt, and mix until smooth. Cover and let it rise for about an hour.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan.
  4. Scoop small portions of the batter using a spoon or your hands and drop them into the hot oil.
  5. Fry until golden brown, drain on paper towels, and serve warm.
